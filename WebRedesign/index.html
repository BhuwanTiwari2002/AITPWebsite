What is AITP?

The Association of Information Technology Professionals (AITP) is a professional association that focuses on information technology education for business professionals. The group is a non-profit US-oriented group, but its activities are performed by 62 local chapters organized on a geographic basis, and 286 student chapters at college and universities.We are the Fox Valley Technical College student chapter.

How can AITP help you?

AITP is a family that is passionate. It helps you grow and experience things you never had.

Events

There Are Different Events That Occur Weekly at AITP. The Events Range from Standard Weekly Meetings, Business Meetings, and Events That Take Place Outside of School. Our AITP Group Aims to be Inclusive to Everyone Regardless of Your Current Skillset. Everyone is Welcome to Take Part of the Meetings! Below is a List of Different Events Coming Up – Please Join Us!

Weekly Standard Meeting Time: Wednesday
4:15 – 5:20 PM
Room: C160 A
Student Speakers: Scheduled Time for Students to Share Interests, Hobbies, or Projects Revolving Around IT.
End of Month Speakers: AITP Wraps Up Every Month with an Outside Speaker. Topics Vary, but There is Always Something for Everyone.
Outside Events: As Our Desire for Knowledge Continues, AITP Attends Events Outside of School. These Include Conference Trips, Conventions, and Volunteering.
Business Meetings: Often AITP Will Engage in Business Meetings. Meetings Are Held to Determine Changes, Schedule, or Events that are Upcoming.
Projects: AITP is Involved in Projects Outside Club Meetings Which are Led by Students, for Students. These Vary But Include Website Design and Game Development.
